``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $\tpl{X,d}$ be a metric space. A sequence $\tpl{x_n}$ in $X$ is said to be **contractive** if there exists some $c\in\l(0,1\r)$ such that_
$$\begin{equation}
    \fa n\in\N:d\l(x_{n+1},x_{n+2}\r)\leq cd\l(x_n,x_{n+1}\r).
\end{equation}$$
```
