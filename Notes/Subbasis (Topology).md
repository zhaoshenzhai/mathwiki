``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $X$ be a set. A **subbasis for a topology on $X$** is a set $\mc{S}\subseteq\pow\l(X\r)$ such that $\mc{S}$ covers $X$. That is, if
$$\begin{equation}
    \fa x\in X:\ex S\in\mc{S}:x\in S.
\end{equation}$$
```
