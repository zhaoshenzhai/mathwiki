``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_A natural number $p\in\N$ with $p>1$ is said to be a **prime number** if the only divisors of $p$ are $1$ and itself._

```

**Remark.** Otherwise, $p$ is said to be a **composite number**.<span style="float:right;">$\blacklozenge$</span>
