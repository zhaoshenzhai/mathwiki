``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $R$ be a binary relation. The **domain and range of $R$** are the sets
$$\begin{equation}
    \dom R\coloneqq\l\{x\mid\ex y:xRy\r\}\ \ \ \ \textrm{\it{and}}\ \ \ \ \ran R\coloneqq\l\{y\mid\ex x:xRy\r\},
\end{equation}$$
respectively.
```
