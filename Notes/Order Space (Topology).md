``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $\tpl{X,<}$ be a totally ordered set. The **(topological) order space on $X$ w.r.t. $<$** is the pair $\tpl{X,\mc{T}\l(<\r)}$ where $\mc{T}\l(<\r)$ is the order topology induced by $<$._

```
