title: Definition.

_A ring $\tpl{R,+,\cdot,0,1}$ is said to be a **division ring** if every non-zero element has a multiplicative inverse; that is, if_
$$\begin{equation}
    \fa r\in R:\l(r\neq0\Rightarrow\ex r^{-1}\in R:r\cdot r^{-1}=1=r^{-1}\cdot r\r).
\end{equation}$$
