``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $V$ and $W$ be $K$-vector spaces. A **$K$-linear isomorphism from $V$ to $W$** is an invertible linear map $T:V\to W$. That is, linear isomorphisms are isomorphisms in $\catvect$._

```
