``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $V$ and $W$ be inner product spaces and let $T:V\to W$ be a linear map. The **adjoint of $T$** is the unique linear map $T^\adj:W\to V$ such that $\inprod{Tv}{w}=\inprod{v}{T^\ast w}$ for all $v\in V$ and $w\in W$.

```
