``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $\tpl{X,d}$ be a metric space. The **diameter of $X$** is_
$$\begin{equation}
    \diam X\coloneqq\sup\limits_{\mathclap{x,y\in X}}d\l(x,y\r),
\end{equation}$$
_where we take $\diam X\coloneqq\infty$ if the supremum does not exist._
```
