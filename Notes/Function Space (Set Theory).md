title: Definition.

_Let $X$ and $Y$ be sets. The **function space of $X$ into $Y$** is the set_
$$\begin{equation}
    \prescript{X}{}{Y}\coloneqq X^Y\coloneqq\l\{f\mid f:X\to Y\r\}.
\end{equation}$$
