``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $v$ and $U$ be sets. The <b>set of relative complements of elements in $U$ w.r.t. $v$</b> is the set
$$\begin{equation}
    \l\{v\comp u\st u\in U\r\}\coloneqq\l\{x\st\l(\ex u\in U\r)x=v\comp u\r\}.
\end{equation}$$
```
