``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $\tpl{X,d}$ be a metric space and fix $S\subseteq X$. Then $S$ is **totally bounded in $X$** if
$$\begin{equation}
    \fa\epsilon>0,\ex n\in\N^+,\ex x_1,\dots,x_n\in S:S\subseteq\bigcup_{i=1}^{n}B_\epsilon\!\l(x_i\r).
\end{equation}$$
```
