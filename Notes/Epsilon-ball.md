``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $\tpl{X,d}$ be a metric space and fix $x\in X$. For all $\epsilon\in\R$, the <b>$\epsilon$-ball centered at $x$</b> is the set
$$\begin{equation}
    B_\epsilon\!\l(x\r)\coloneqq\l\{y\in X\st d\l(x,y\r)<\epsilon\r\}.
\end{equation}$$
```
