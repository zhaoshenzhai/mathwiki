``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $\tpl{X,d}$ be a metric space and fix $x\in X$. For all $\epsilon\in\R$, the **$\epsilon$-ball centered at $x$** is the set_
$$\begin{equation}
    B_\epsilon\!\l(x\r)\coloneqq\l\{y\in X\mid d\l(x,y\r)<\epsilon\r\}.
\end{equation}$$
```
