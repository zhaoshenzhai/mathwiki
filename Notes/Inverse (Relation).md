``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $R$ be a binary relation. The **inverse of $R$** is the relation_
$$\begin{equation}
    R^{-1}\coloneqq\l\{\l\langle y,x\r\rangle\mid xRy\r\}.
\end{equation}$$

```

**Remark.** Observe that $xRy\Leftrightarrow yR^{-1}x$.<span style="float:right;">$\blacklozenge$</span>
