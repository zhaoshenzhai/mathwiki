\begin{definition}

A topological space $X$ is _separable_ if $X$ admits a countable dense subset.

\end{definition}

&emsp;&emsp;More generally, we say that $X$ is _$\kappa$-separable_ if it admits a dense subset of cardinality $\kappa$.
