``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $G$ be a group acting on a set $X$ and fix $x\in X$. The <b>stabilizer/isotropy subgroup of $x$ under $G$</b> is the subgroup $G_x\coloneqq\l\{g\in G\st gx=x\r\}$.

```
