Ideal

To study the quotient objects in a category, we must first understand the congruence relations in said category. For $\catring$, these are in correspondence with ideals.

Definition.

Let $R$ be a ring. A (left) ideal in $R$ is a subgroup $(\mf{a},+)\substructeq R$ such that $r\mf{a}\subseteq\mf{a}$ for all $r\in R$.


  Similarly for right ideals and two-sided ideals.
